# LawScape Legal Ontology v1.0

## Overview

The LawScape Legal Ontology defines the structure of the Living Legal Knowledge Graph (LLKG).

It specifies:

- Legal entities (nodes)
- Relationships (edges)
- Entity properties
- Relationship properties
- Temporal versioning
- Graph constraints

This ontology serves as the foundation for:

- Knowledge Graph Construction
- Legal Ecosystem Impact Analysis (LEIA)
- Temporal Judgment Dependency Analysis (TJDA)
- Explainable Legal Intelligence

---

## Design Principles

The ontology is designed according to the following principles:

1. Modularity
2. Explainability
3. Temporal Awareness
4. Scalability
5. Research Originality
6. Graph-based Reasoning
7. Version Awareness
