# LawScape Project Specification

## Vision

LawScape is a Living Legal Intelligence Platform that transforms fragmented legal information into a Temporal Heterogeneous Knowledge Graph for explainable legal reasoning, legal ecosystem impact analysis, and version-aware judicial dependency analysis.

## Goals

- Build a Living Legal Knowledge Graph
- Model legal evolution over time
- Perform Legal Ecosystem Impact Analysis (LEIA)
- Perform Temporal Judgment Dependency Analysis (TJDA)
- Generate Explainable Legal Intelligence

## Development Principles

- Research First
- Explainability
- Modularity
- Scalability
- Reproducibility
- Production Quality
